<div>
<p><strong>A console application that will conjugate Japanese verbs, adjectives and nouns. Get kanji infomation, download audio of pronunciations, various tests and practice, a translator &amp; sentence breakdown and more!</strong>&nbsp;<a href="https://imgur.com/DlKXeyl" rel="nofollow">Here</a>&nbsp;you can see the program performing its main function (Word Lookup). Learn japanese with this amazing tool :)</p>
</div>
<div>
<p><strong>Download the program&nbsp;<a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/releases">here</a>.</strong>&nbsp;The interval that I release a new version will be bigger now because I am back to being a student with lots of other work I need to be focused on, not to fear though, the project isn't dead.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p><strong>For the main functionality of bringing up conjugations of a word, simple type the word on it's own (in Japanese, Romaji or the English word). Find out more about how this and other commands work in&nbsp;<a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use">the Wiki</a>.</strong></p>

<h2><strong>Summary of&nbsp;<a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#list-of-commands">commands</a>:</strong></h2>
</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li><strong><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#word-lookup">Word Lookup</a></strong>&nbsp;- Looks up any word, phrase or sentence</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#translator">Translator</a></strong>&nbsp;- Translates English into Japanese, or Japanese into English</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#kanji-information">Kanji Information</a></strong>&nbsp;- Enter Japanese text and detailed information on all the kanji</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#audio">Audio</a></strong>&nbsp;- Downloads audio of various conjugations of a verb or adjective</li>
<li>HeyLingo Audio (/heylingo) - Downloads audio from&nbsp;<a href="https://www.heylingo.com/" rel="nofollow">Hey! Lingo</a>&nbsp;courses</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#listening">Listening</a></strong>&nbsp;- Start a listening test for a verb or adjective</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#kanji-test">Kanji Test</a></strong>&nbsp;- Enter Japanese text and get tested on all the kanji</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#prefs-command">Preferences</a></strong>&nbsp;- Lets you change program settings, these are saved for the next time you use the program.</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#conjugation-practice"><strong>Conjugator Practice</strong></a>&nbsp;- conjugation practice (practice conjugating a word of your choice into different forms)</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#last-requests">Last Requests</a></strong>&nbsp;- Things you can do after searching a word</li>
<li><strong><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#s-parameter">S parameter</a></strong>&nbsp;- A parameter that determines how much information about a word is displayed</li>
<li><strong>/history</strong>&nbsp;- See you last 40 searches</li>
<li><strong>/b (back)</strong>&nbsp;- Instantly go back to your last search (no internet connection needed)</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#help-command">/h or /help</a>&nbsp;- Brings up more information about a command</li>
<li><a href="https://github.com/hopto-dot/Japanese-Conjugation-Helper/wiki/How-to-use#reading-practice">Reading Practice</a>&nbsp;- Reading practice (obsolete command)</li>
<li>/ranki - Reverse Anki: Generate reverse anki cards (obsolete command)</li>
</ul>
